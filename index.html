About Me

Meet Enoch & Grey Mountain Mechanical

Based in Utah, Grey Mountain Mechanical provides top-tier mechanical and plumbing design solutions for complex commercial, light industrial, and hospitality projects. Led by Enoch, we specialize in high-efficiency load calculations, custom HVAC air-handling architectures, and high-precision sanitary plumbing designs.

Every project is built upon rigorous direct coordination with architects, general contractors, and owners to guarantee that spaces remain seamlessly ventilated, functional, and fully code-compliant.
